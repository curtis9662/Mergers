## Detailed System Profiles

### 1. Epic Systems (Epic Willow)
* **Overview:** Epic is the undisputed leader in the US healthcare EHR market, utilizing a single centralized database for all patient records.
* **Pharmacy Integration:** The **Epic Willow** module connects inpatient and outpatient pharmacy workflows flawlessly with the broader clinical EHR, eliminating dangerous data silos and preventing medication errors.
* **Key Strengths:** 
  * Advanced Electronic Prescribing for Controlled Substances (EPCS).
  * Native Prescription Drug Monitoring Program (PDMP) integration.
  * Industry-leading interoperability via *Care Everywhere*.

### 2. Oracle Health (Cerner Millennium & PharmNet)
* **Overview:** Now backed by Oracle's enterprise infrastructure, Cerner provides robust cloud-based EHR solutions serving a massive portion of US health networks.
* **Pharmacy Integration:** **Cerner PharmNet** automates the entire medication pipeline—from the point of prescribing through administration and billing.
* **Key Strengths:**
  * High-precision Barcode Medication Administration (BCMA).
  * Sophisticated drug-allergy and drug-drug interaction alerts.
  * Deep integration with automated dispensing machines.

### 3. Meditech (Expanse Pharmacy)
* **Overview:** Meditech is an industry staple for mid-sized, rural, and community hospitals, offering a highly functional and financially accessible EHR suite (Meditech Expanse).
* **Pharmacy Integration:** Connects prescribers, pharmacists, and nurses in a unified ecosystem, streamlining the medication order review process.
* **Key Strengths:**
  * Centralized medication reconciliation.
  * Smart dispensing cabinet support.
  * Automated dose calculation and robust clinical validation tools.

### 4. eClinicalWorks
* **Overview:** A powerhouse in the ambulatory space, eClinicalWorks is specifically tailored for high-volume outpatient practices and multi-specialty clinics.
* **Pharmacy Integration:** While not a traditional hospital pharmacy system, it features a deeply integrated electronic prescribing architecture connecting directly to over 98,000 retail pharmacies across the US.
* **Key Strengths:**
  * Seamless telehealth prescription generation without disruptive manual data transfers.
  * Mobile applications focused on patient medication adherence.
  * Extensive custom workflows for specialty prescribing patterns.

### 5. PrimeRx
* **Overview:** Unlike the hospital-centric EHRs, PrimeRx is an award-winning Pharmacy Management and EHR system designed *specifically* for the pharmacy sector.
* **Pharmacy Integration:** Serves retail, specialty, compounding, and mail-order pharmacies by pulling in patient medical data and optimizing outbound care.
* **Key Strengths:**
  * Automated inventory control and immediate stock-shortage responses.
  * Easy integration with major clinical EHRs via APIs.
  * Built-in tools for patient loyalty, home delivery, and curbside pickup management.
