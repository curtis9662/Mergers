### 🎯 Pharma Printer Security Attestation Questionnaire

This advanced attestation questionnaire is specifically designed to enforce enterprise cybersecurity configuration best practices and strictly restrict printer use to business purposes in a pharmaceutical prescription-handling environment. 🛡️💊✨

The workbook is organized into four core tabs:

* 📊 **Dashboard & Summary:** A brief, executive overview of the domains and question counts. 📈
* 🏗️ **Security Architecture:** Focuses on network segmentation, Zero Trust Access (ZTNA), data encryption in transit/at rest, IAM/RBAC integration, and physical security parameters for multi-function printers handling PHI/PCI. 🔐🔥
* ⚙️ **Security Engineering:** Covers the technical deployment and hardening controls. This includes disabling legacy protocols (AppSocket/Telnet), enforcing firmware updates, active Data Loss Prevention (DLP), strict session timeouts, and port (USB) restrictions. 💻🚀
* ⚖️ **GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance):** Focuses on Acceptable Use Policies (AUP), HIPAA/PCI-DSS specific training for ePHI/physical PHI handling, breach incident response protocols, and third-party vendor (MPS) BAA enforcement. 📋💯

✅ **Streamlined Auditing:** The *Response* columns feature built-in data validation drop-downs (*Yes, No, N/A, In Progress*) to seamlessly perform assessments and easily document corrective actions. 🎯🙌
